How much does this all cost?

The price of building, designing & creating a website completely depends on the spec of the site. The process which I follow is the arrange for us to meet in person over a coffee to discuss the design in detail and what you are looking to achieve. Once this is done, I will send you a price via email or text which will include everything and is a final price including VAT. All I ask for is a 20% deposit to begin the project and this is non-refundable as I will fully concretartate on only your project and this will involve me taking considerable tme out of my day to do so. If you would like to enquire, please do not hesitate to contact me.

How soon can you create me a website?

I can create a website within 1 week to 8 months dependant upon the spec, complexity and my work schedule (which can be very busy!) however I will always go through this when we talk or meet so you can have it completed within reasonable time.

What if I decide I no longer want a website once gone through the process?

No problem at all, just let me know that you have decided you no longer want a website and I can send you the files over incase you change your mind down the line and I am more than happy to go back to it at a later date if requested. The only cost which will be lost is the 20% deposit which would of been paid prior to starting the site.
